A new chapter has unfolded for **JSM Cooperative Corporation**.

With the debut of **The Man in the Ball Cap**, we introduced readers to a mystery rooted in place, memory, atmosphere, and purpose.

Set against the vibrant backdrop of **A Coruña, Galicia**, the story follows **Pepe Miguel**, a private investigator drawn into the city’s darker edges — a world of secrets, symbols, hidden motives, and unanswered questions.

But the novel is more than a mystery.

It is also the beginning of a larger adventure for JSM Cooperative: a journey to use storytelling as a way to gather community, support meaningful causes, and invite readers into something bigger than the page.

---

## A mystery begins in A Coruña

A Coruña is not just a setting in *The Man in the Ball Cap*.

It is part of the atmosphere.

The city gives the story its texture: the Atlantic air, the old streets, the coastline, the shadows, the history, and the sense that every corner might be hiding a clue.

Through Pepe Miguel’s investigation, readers step into a world where mystery and place are inseparable.

The story asks readers to follow the signs, question appearances, and keep walking forward — even when the path becomes uncertain.

That spirit is deeply connected to what JSM Cooperative would eventually call **The Camino**.

---

## From our hands to yours

As the book began reaching readers, one of the most meaningful parts of the journey was seeing people connect with the story directly.

Signed copies, chapter readings, reader reactions, and social updates helped turn the novel into something more personal.

A book can be purchased quietly.

But a signed book feels different.

It carries a message.

It becomes a shared object between writer, reader, and mission.

That is part of what we want JSM Cooperative to be: not just a publisher of stories, but a builder of connections around those stories.

## Join us on the Camino

Our journey does not end with storytelling.

From the beginning, JSM Cooperative has been built around the idea that creative work can support meaningful causes.

The mission is simple:

> Write stories. Build community. Support impact.

That mission has grown into **The Camino** — the shared path where readers, donors, writers, nonprofits, and community members walk together.

The Camino is where book updates, chapter readings, community campaigns, donations, newsletters, and nonprofit-aligned work all come together.

By joining us, you are not just following a book.

You are joining a growing community dedicated to changing lives one story at a time.

## More than a book launch

Looking back, this moment was about more than announcing a novel.

It was about beginning a mission.

*The Man in the Ball Cap* gave JSM Cooperative its first major creative anchor. It helped us start asking bigger questions:

- Can a mystery novel become part of a fundraising model?
- Can readers become supporters?
- Can storytelling help nonprofits flourish?
- Can a book become the beginning of a community?
- Can creative work become a Camino?

Those questions still guide us.

And we are still walking toward the answers.
