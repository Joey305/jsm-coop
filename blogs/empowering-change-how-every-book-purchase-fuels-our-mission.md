At **JSM Cooperative Corporation**, every story we tell is part of a larger mission.

With the release of our debut novel, **The Man in the Ball Cap**, we began a journey that is not only literary, but community-driven. This book is more than a creative project. It is one of the first ways we are testing a bigger idea:

> Can storytelling become a structure for helping people?

We believe the answer is yes.

Every copy purchased, every reader who shares the story, and every supporter who joins the Camino helps move that mission forward.

---

## A partnership rooted in purpose

JSM Cooperative was created to connect creative work with meaningful impact.

One of the earliest and most important parts of that mission is our connection with **Mindful Diabetes Inc.**, a nonprofit focused on education, awareness, and community-centered conversations around diabetes, metabolic health, Alzheimer’s risk, and the idea of Type 3 diabetes.

[Learn More About Mindful Diabetes →](https://mindfuldiabetes.org/){: .article-btn .article-btn-outline }

Through this connection, *The Man in the Ball Cap* becomes more than a book.

It becomes a vehicle for awareness.

It becomes a conversation starter.

It becomes a way for readers to participate in something larger than the story itself.

---

## The journey of every purchase

Every book purchased helps support the growing ecosystem behind JSM Cooperative.

That includes:

- publishing and campaign costs
- community storytelling work
- nonprofit-aligned education and outreach
- future creative-impact projects
- tools that help readers, donors, and supporters stay connected
- the continued development of the Camino as a community pathway

A purchase is not just a transaction.

It is a step.

A step toward proving that books can do more than sit on shelves. They can gather people, move resources, and help build a community around shared purpose.

## Why this matters

The goal of JSM Cooperative is not simply to publish books.

The goal is to build a model where creative work can support community work.

That means every campaign has two sides:

1. **The story itself** — the book, the mystery, the characters, the emotional journey.
2. **The impact behind it** — the readers, supporters, nonprofit partners, and community projects that grow around the work.

*The Man in the Ball Cap* is the first major step in building that model.

It gives us a story to gather around, but it also gives us a practical way to test how storytelling can generate support, awareness, and momentum.

---

## Looking back and moving forward

When we first released *The Man in the Ball Cap*, we were not only watching book performance.

We were watching a mission begin to take shape.

Every reader response, every purchase, every shared post, and every conversation gave us insight into what JSM Cooperative could become.

That feedback continues to shape the road ahead.

It helps us ask better questions:

- How can we make the book easier to discover?
- How can we connect readers more directly to the mission?
- How can we turn one-time purchases into long-term community?
- How can we help nonprofit partners through creative campaigns?
- How can we make the Camino something people truly feel part of?

The answers will keep evolving, but the direction is clear.

We are building a path where stories and service move together.
