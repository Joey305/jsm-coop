JSM Cooperative Corporation has reached a major milestone.

We are proud to share that **JSM Cooperative Corporation has officially received tax-exempt recognition**.

This moment marks a new chapter in our story — not only as a publishing organization, but as a mission-driven platform built around the belief that creative work can help support meaningful causes.

This recognition is more than paperwork.

It is a foundation.

It is a signal that the work we are building has a purpose larger than any single book, campaign, or update.

It is another step forward on the Camino.

---

## What this milestone means

Receiving tax-exempt recognition gives JSM Cooperative a stronger structure for the work ahead.

It helps formalize the mission we have been building from the beginning:

> To create stories, books, campaigns, and creative projects that support nonprofit-aligned impact.

For us, storytelling is not separate from service.

Publishing is not separate from purpose.

Books are not just products.

They are tools for gathering people, moving resources, and building community around causes that matter.

This milestone gives us a stronger foundation to keep doing that work with more clarity, accountability, and long-term direction.

---

## A literary mission with a larger purpose

JSM Cooperative was created around a simple idea:

**Stories can fund a better world.**

That means our creative work is designed to do more than entertain. We want our books, blogs, campaigns, newsletters, and community updates to become part of a larger ecosystem of support.

Our mission includes:

- writing and publishing books across multiple genres
- using creative campaigns to support nonprofit-aligned work
- building community around storytelling and service
- helping readers understand the impact behind the stories
- creating a platform where art, education, and generosity can meet

This tax-exempt recognition strengthens that mission.

It allows us to continue building JSM Cooperative as a home for storytelling-as-solidarity.

---

## The role of *The Man in the Ball Cap*

Our debut novel, **The Man in the Ball Cap**, sits at the center of this first chapter.

The book is not only a mystery novel.

It is also one of the first practical expressions of the JSM Cooperative model: using creative work to invite people into a larger mission.

Through the book, readers can support the Co-op, discover the story, follow the Camino, and participate in a growing community of people who believe literature can do something meaningful.
