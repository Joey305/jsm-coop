Dear friends, readers, and supporters,

As the release of **The Man in the Ball Cap** approached, we wanted to pause and say something simple:

**Thank you.**

Your encouragement, curiosity, shares, conversations, and belief in the mission helped carry JSM Cooperative through one of its earliest and most meaningful chapters.

A book launch is never just about the book.

It is about the people who believe in it before the world fully sees it.

It is about the community that gathers around the story.

It is about the shared hope that something creative can become something useful, generous, and lasting.

---

## A new chapter together

The release of **The Man in the Ball Cap** marked a major step for JSM Cooperative Corporation.

This was not only the beginning of a novel’s public life.

It was the beginning of a larger mission: using storytelling to support community-centered impact and nonprofit-aligned work.

From the earliest days, this project was built with a bigger question in mind:

> Can a story become a source of support?

That question continues to guide us.

Every reader, every supporter, and every person who shared the work helped us move closer to answering it.

## Gratitude at the heart of the mission

The holiday season always reminds us of what matters most.

Family.

Memory.

Health.

Connection.

Time.

The people we love.

The moments we do not want to take for granted.

Those themes sit close to the heart of JSM Cooperative. They also connect deeply to the work of **Mindful Diabetes Inc.**, a nonprofit focused on education and awareness around diabetes, metabolic health, Alzheimer’s risk, and the idea often discussed as Type 3 diabetes.

Through JSM Cooperative, we hope to build a creative platform where books, blogs, donations, newsletters, and campaigns can help support work like this.

## The Man in the Ball Cap as a symbol

**The Man in the Ball Cap** is more than our debut novel.

It is the first major creative anchor in the JSM Cooperative mission.

It represents the belief that stories can:

- gather people
- spark conversations
- support meaningful causes
- invite readers into a larger community
- become part of something beyond the page

The book gave us a place to begin.

The community gave us a reason to continue.

## Carrying gratitude into the new year

As we stepped toward the new year, the feeling was both reflective and hopeful.

We were grateful for what had already happened.

We were excited for what was coming next.

And we were aware that this journey would only grow if people continued to walk it with us.

That is why the Camino matters.

The Camino is not only a campaign phrase. It is the shared path behind JSM Cooperative — the path where readers, donors, writers, nonprofits, collaborators, and community members walk together.

Every post, every book, every donation, every newsletter signup, and every shared update becomes part of that road.
