**The Man in the Ball Cap** is the debut novel connected to **JSM Cooperative Corporation** — and the first major creative work at the center of our mission to turn storytelling into community-powered impact.

At JSM Cooperative, we believe stories can do more than entertain.

They can move people.

They can gather support.

They can help fund meaningful work.

They can become a Camino — a shared path walked by readers, supporters, families, friends, and communities who believe that creative work can carry something bigger than itself.

---

## A novel at the center of the mission

*The Man in the Ball Cap* is more than a book launch.

It is the creative anchor for JSM Cooperative’s first major storytelling campaign: a campaign built around books, reader support, nonprofit-aligned impact, and the belief that art can become a vehicle for generosity.

The novel sits at the intersection of:

- memory
- mystery
- place
- purpose
- community
- impact

It is a story readers can enter, but it is also a project supporters can participate in.

Every copy purchased, every update shared, every signed book sent, and every reader who joins the journey helps us build a larger model for storytelling-as-solidarity.

## Why this book matters to JSM Cooperative

JSM Cooperative was created to explore a simple but powerful idea:

> Creative work can become a structure for helping people.

That means our books are not isolated projects. They are part of a broader ecosystem of publishing, fundraising, education, and community support.

*The Man in the Ball Cap* helps us begin that work publicly.

It gives readers a story to connect with, while also giving supporters a way to participate in something larger: a cooperative model where creative work helps fund mission-aligned impact.

## The Camino behind the book

The Camino is the symbolic path behind JSM Cooperative’s reader-powered community.

It represents movement, reflection, generosity, and the idea that people can walk together toward something meaningful.

Through the Camino, a single book becomes more than a purchase.

It becomes:

- a gift
- a conversation
- a fundraiser
- a community signal
- a shared step forward

That is why *The Man in the Ball Cap* is tied to our **Join The Camino** novel subscription.
