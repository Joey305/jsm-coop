How Your Donation Travels the Camino

1 · You Give
You make a donation through our secure PayPal gateway. That gift lands directly with the JSM Cooperative Corporation, where it’s used with intention and care.

2 · We Create
Your donation helps us write, edit, design, and publish novels like The Man in the Ballcap—books built to entertain, move hearts, and spark conversation.

3 · The Stories Travel
Those books reach readers, book clubs, communities, and campaigns. Each copy becomes a tiny messenger for the work of our nonprofit partners.

4 · Profits Become Support
100% of novel profits are donated to nonprofits we’ve aligned with the UN SDGs—starting with Mindful Diabetes Inc. and their work exploring the connections between Type II and Type III (Alzheimer’s) diabetes.

5 · The Impact Echoes
A reader feels seen. A nonprofit gets a boost. A researcher has more runway. Someone receives education, care, or community they otherwise wouldn’t have.

Your donation is not a one-time act—it’s the first step in a Camino that winds through stories, into lives, and out into a future we’re building together.
