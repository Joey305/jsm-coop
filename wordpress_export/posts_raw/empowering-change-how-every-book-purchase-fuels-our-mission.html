At the JSM Cooperative Corporation, every story we tell is a step towards a greater cause. With the release of our debut novel, "The Man in the Ballcap," we embarked on a journey not just of literary exploration but of impactful change. 📚✨

A Partnership for Change

In partnership with Mindful Diabetes Inc., we've turned pages into action. This collaboration is at the heart of our mission, blending the art of storytelling with the fight against Alzheimer's. By choosing to purchase our novel, you're directly contributing to innovative research and support programs aimed at understanding and combating this disease. It's a partnership that exemplifies our belief in the power of literature to drive societal progress. 🤝💡

The Journey of Every Purchase

Every book purchased is a beacon of hope. We meticulously funnel the proceeds towards supporting not only Alzheimer's research through our allies at Mindful Diabetes Inc. but also other nonprofits aligned with our vision. The selection process for our nonprofit beneficiaries is thorough and thoughtful, ensuring that your contributions make the broadest impact possible. We look for organizations that not only align with our goals but also embody the spirit of innovation and compassion that we hold dear. 💖🌍

The funds will Be instrumental in various capacities - from sponsoring educational initiatives that demystify Type 2 and Type 3 Diabetes to fostering community support networks for those navigating the complexities of Alzheimer's. Each dollar spent on "The Man in the Ballcap" reverberates through these initiatives, amplifying our collective impact on the world.

Looking Back and Moving Forward

As we look to the future, we are excited about the possibilities that lie ahead - from expanding our literary repertoire to forging new partnerships that amplify our mission.
