Meet the JSM Cooperative Team

We are a collective of storytellers, editors, designers, dreamers, and community builders, working together as one unit. There are no titles that sit above others here — just a cooperative of humans using fiction, imagination, and heart to fund a better world.

Cooperative, not corporate Powered by the UN SDGs Stories that fund change #JOINTHECAMINO
J. Michael Schulz
Founder · Author · Camino Architect

A man of many hats, J. Michael imagines the JSM Cooperative as a story engine that can breathe life into nonprofits across all 17 UN Sustainable Development Goals. From his days leading GenUN at Rollins College to launching The Man in the Ballcap, he is obsessed with turning fiction into fuel for real-world change.

Alongside the team, he brings characters and causes to life — hoping to captivate a large, diverse community by writing across genres while keeping one promise: 100% of novel profits go back into the world.

Jack is the first set of eyes every story meets. From clunky commas to tangled plot threads, he quietly removes every distraction before a reader ever turns the page. A storyteller in his own right, he knows how to keep dialogue sharp, characters grounded, and pacing honest.

His work lives between the lines — making sure every sentence serves the bigger mission: stories that readers love and nonprofits can grow from.
