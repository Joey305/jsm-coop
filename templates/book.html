About the novel

The Man in the Ball Cap is more than a debut novel. It is the first creative anchor in JSM Cooperative’s larger effort to turn books, stories, and community participation into lasting impact.

  • 🎭
    Literary mystery: A story shaped by memory, place, identity, and the search for meaning.
  • 🚶
    Camino-inspired purpose: A creative journey built around reflection, solidarity, and forward movement.
  • 💙
    Impact-centered publishing: Purchases and donations help support mission-aligned nonprofit work.
