About JSM Cooperative

A cooperative home for stories, service, and impact.

JSM Cooperative Corporation is built around a simple but powerful belief: creative work can do more than entertain. It can organize people, raise money, create community, and point attention toward meaningful causes.

Our mission

We publish and promote creative work that supports nonprofit impact. Our debut project, The Man in the Ball Cap, is part of a broader effort to connect readers, writers, donors, and community members through story-driven action.

The goal is not just to sell a book. The goal is to build a repeatable model where storytelling becomes a sustainable engine for generosity.

Our philosophy

  • Stories matter. They make people feel, remember, and act.
  • Community matters. Readers are not passive; they are part of the Camino.
  • Transparency matters. Impact should be clear, trackable, and honest.
  • Independence matters. The new site is built to be controlled by us, not locked inside WordPress.
